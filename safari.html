National Parks

Safaris in Sri Lanka offer a chance to see diverse wildlife, including elephants, leopards, and numerous bird species. Popular national parks include Yala, Udawalawe, Wilpattu, and Minneriya, each known for different animals. Jeep safaris are common, with the dry season (May to September) being the best time to visit. These safaris also provide excellent wildlife photography opportunities and support conservation efforts.
