Little Adam's Peak

Little Adam's Peak, also known as Punchi Sri Pada, is a popular hiking destination in Ella, Sri Lanka. Named after the sacred Adam's Peak due to the similarity in shape, it offers stunning views and a relatively easy hike, making it accessible to all age groups.

The hike to Little Adam's Peak begins from the town of Ella, winding through lush tea plantations and offering breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys and hills. The path is well-marked and the hike itself is not very strenuous, making it a perfect activity for families and casual hikers.

At the summit, hikers are rewarded with panoramic views of the Ella Gap and the surrounding mountains. The cool breeze and the serene environment at the top provide a perfect spot to relax and take in the natural beauty of the area.

Little Adam's Peak is not just about the hike and the views; it also offers an insight into the rural life of Sri Lanka. The journey through the tea plantations provides an opportunity to interact with the local tea pickers and learn about the tea industry in Sri Lanka.

Whether you are looking for a peaceful hike, stunning views, or a cultural experience, Little Adam's Peak has something to offer. It is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Ella.
