diving in Night Snorkeling Mirissa

Add a dose of undersea adventure to your Sri Lanka vacation with this private night snorkel in Mirissa. Ideal for novice and seasoned snorkelers wanting to try something different, you’ll enter the sea after dark to explore the underwater world. Accompanied by instructors and flashlights, spot nocturnal creatures like turtles, lobsters, and octopus—which tend to hide during daylight—and waft over colorful corals—a magical experience. Provided equipment, training, and hotel transfers from nearby resorts make everything hassle-free.
