About Us

Welcome to Ceylon Extreme Adventures, your number one source for all things adventure in Sri Lanka. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of adventure tourism, with a focus on reliability, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2024 by Supun Ravidu, Umesh Sathsarana, and Devmin Senevirathna, Ceylon Extreme Adventures has come a long way from its beginnings in Colombo. When we first started out, our passion for promoting adventure tourism drove us to do tons of research so that Ceylon Extreme Adventures can offer you the world's most advanced adventure tourism platform. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We hope you enjoy our services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,

Supun Ravidu, Umesh Sathsarana, Devmin Senevirathna
