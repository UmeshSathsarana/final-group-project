Alagalla Mountain Range

The Alagalla Mountain Range, also known as the Potato Range, is a scenic and adventurous destination located in the Central Province of Sri Lanka. Known for its rugged beauty and panoramic views, it is a favorite spot for hikers and nature enthusiasts.

The hike to Alagalla's summit is moderately challenging, taking you through lush forests, tea plantations, and rocky terrain. The trail is well-marked, and the ascent offers stunning vistas of the surrounding countryside, making it a rewarding experience for those who love the outdoors.

At the peak, visitors are greeted with breathtaking views of the Central Highlands and the surrounding valleys. The cool breeze and the serene environment provide a perfect backdrop for relaxation and reflection.

The Alagalla Mountain Range is not just about the hike; it also offers an opportunity to explore the local flora and fauna. The area is home to various species of birds and other wildlife, making it a great destination for nature lovers and bird watchers.

Whether you are looking for a challenging hike, stunning views, or a chance to connect with nature, the Alagalla Mountain Range has something to offer. It is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to the Central Province of Sri Lanka.
