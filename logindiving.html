Diving

A diving adventure involves exploring underwater environments using scuba gear, which includes a mask, snorkel, fins, a wetsuit, buoyancy control device (BCD), regulator, and an air tank. This equipment allows divers to breathe underwater and adjust their buoyancy so they can float, hover, or sink as needed.
