Upper Diyaluma

Upper Diyaluma is a stunning waterfall located in the Badulla District of Sri Lanka. Known for its breathtaking views and serene environment, it is a popular destination for nature lovers and hikers.

The hike to Upper Diyaluma offers a moderately challenging trail through lush forests and picturesque landscapes. The trail is well-marked, providing hikers with an enjoyable and rewarding experience.

At the top of the waterfall, visitors are rewarded with panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. The cool breeze and the tranquil environment make it an ideal spot for relaxation and reflection.

Upper Diyaluma is not just about the hike; it also offers an opportunity to explore the rich biodiversity of the region. The area is home to various species of flora and fauna, making it a great destination for nature lovers.

Whether you are an experienced hiker or someone looking for a peaceful retreat, Upper Diyaluma is a must-visit destination. It promises an unforgettable experience with its unique blend of natural beauty and serenity.
