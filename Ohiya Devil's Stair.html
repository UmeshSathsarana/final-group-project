Ohiya Devil's Stair

Ohiya Devil's Stair is a thrilling and adventurous trail located in the Central Province of Sri Lanka. Known for its steep and challenging path, it offers an exciting experience for hikers and adventure seekers.

The trail features a series of steep steps and rugged terrain, leading hikers through dense forests and scenic landscapes. The journey is demanding but rewarding, with stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys along the way.

At various points along the trail, hikers can take in the breathtaking vistas of the Central Highlands. The cool climate and the natural beauty of the area provide a perfect setting for an exhilarating adventure.

Ohiya Devil's Stair is not just about the thrill of the hike; it also offers an opportunity to explore the rich biodiversity of the region. The trail is home to a variety of flora and fauna, making it a great destination for nature enthusiasts.

Whether you are an experienced hiker or someone looking for a challenging adventure, Ohiya Devil's Stair is a must-visit destination. It promises an unforgettable experience with its unique blend of natural beauty and adrenaline-pumping excitement.
